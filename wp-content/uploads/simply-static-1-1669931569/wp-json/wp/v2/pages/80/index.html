Gabriel Patr\u00edcio<\/strong> (b. 1996) was born and raised in Guarda, Portugal and he is currently based in The Hague.<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

The passion for analogue photography arose along-with the need to escape the overwhelming immediate world. Amazed by the whole process – learning the camera settings, nailing the exposure triangle and the simple fact that you need to wait for your pictures to come to life are the perfect composition for making every shot count and memorable.<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

From landscapes to portraits, from the concrete to the abstract, his shots are still the result of a constant seeking for his artistic personality, nonetheless a persistent melancholic warmth can be found in every picture.\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n
